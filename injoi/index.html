06 - The Bitter Truth

Behind Conventional Chocolate

4 facts the chocolate industry doesn't want you to know

01

Heat-Processed Cacao = Lost Nutrients

Most commercial chocolate uses high heat, destroying antioxidants, flavonoids, and other beneficial natural compounds.

02

White Sugar - Sweet but Toxic

Regular chocolate is loaded with refined white sugar, which stresses the liver, spikes blood sugar, and promotes inflammation.

03

Cheap Chocolate Comes at a Human Cost

Mass-market brands often source cacao from farms that rely on underpaid or even child labor in developing countries.

04

Artificial Additives and Preservatives

Many chocolates are packed with artificial flavors, fragrances, and emulsifiers - none of which belong in your body.
