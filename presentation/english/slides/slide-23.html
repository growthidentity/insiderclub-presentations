2. BINARY BONUS
Binary Bonus
What is it and how does it work?
WHAT IS BINARY?
Your team splits into two sides:
YOU
LEFT SIDE
RIGHT SIDE
Each new member goes to either left or right side
HOW TO ACTIVATE?
Binary activates when you have:
1
Active contract + capital
2
1 direct on left side
3
1 direct on right side
Direct = person you personally invited
Requirements met → BINARY ACTIVE
HOW DOES IT PAY?
Based on WEAKER side
Left $10K, right $8K → commission on $8K
Each new contract in your team gives up to 10% binary bonus:
1-4 qualifiers: each gets 10%
5+ qualifiers: sponsor 10%, rest share 40% pool
EXAMPLE: New contract $1,000
$1,000 × 10% = $100
binary bonus based on weaker side
Bigger team, more contracts, bigger binary.
