5. ROYALTY MATCHING
Royalty Matching Rule
Understanding how royalty passes through your team structure
Key Rule to Understand
If someone in your downline qualifies for the same royalty % as you...
They receive it, NOT you!
Royalty passes to the closest qualified person
To earn royalty, you must outrank those below you
How to Earn Royalty
Rank higher than your downline members to earn the difference
Your rank earns: 12%
Downline rank earns: 10%
You receive: +2%
