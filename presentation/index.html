Know Your Broker

A-Book vs B-Book Brokers

This is the most important thing most traders don't know. Your broker's model determines whether they want you to win or lose.

B-Book Broker Avoid

The broker takes the opposite side of your trade. When you lose, they profit. They are literally betting against you.

  • Profits when you lose money
  • Conflict of interest
  • May manipulate prices
  • ~90% of brokers operate this way

A-Book Broker Choose This

The broker passes your orders to liquidity providers. They earn from spreads and commissions - not your losses.

  • Earns from spreads, not your losses
  • No conflict of interest
  • Transparent execution
  • Wants you to keep trading (winning)
