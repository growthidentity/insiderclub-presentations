The Problem with Current System

Why It's Unbalanced

  • Only HUB counts for rank volume
  • Capital (5.45x larger) is completely ignored
  • Big investors buy large hubs once, then only add capital
  • Teams look 5-10x smaller than actual business
  • Beginners can't grow - need to "sell hubs" not contracts
  • Leaders demotivated - their real volume isn't reflected

What Works Well

  • Hub = contract fee (pure company revenue)
  • Capital = real business money for arbitrage
  • Commissions paid from Hub (not capital) - protects liquidity
  • 5X cap rule limits exposure
  • Royalty requires weekly maintenance (2% business)
  • Hub must be activated before commissions trigger
Proposed: New Volume Structure

RANK VOLUME CALCULATION (FOR QUALIFICATION ONLY)

1 Hub $ = 1 VP   |   1 Capital $ = 0.25 VP

VP = Volume Points for rank qualification only. Commission payouts remain unchanged (Hub only).

Why 0.25 for Capital?
  • Capital is NOT free money - company uses it for arbitrage operations
  • Company pays 0.165-5% daily ROI from capital profits
  • But capital represents real customer commitment and business growth
  • Including it at 25% is conservative and reflects actual business flow
  • Hub to Capacity ratio is ~5.45x, so capital is naturally larger
