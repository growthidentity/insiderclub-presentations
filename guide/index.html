Part 1
Registration & KYC Verification
1
Register on AITECH IB Portal
CLICK HERE and fill out the registration form: first name, last name, email, password, country and phone number. Check both risk warning boxes and click Register.
2
Verify Email (IB Portal)
You will receive an email from the IB Portal. Click the "Verify Account" button to confirm your email address. The link expires after 4320 minutes.
3
Email Confirmed
After clicking the link, you will see the "emailVerified" confirmation message. Click "continueToLogin".
4
Log In to AITECH Portal
Enter your email and password and click "Login".
5
Register TAG Markets from IB Portal
After logging in, scroll down until you find the "Tag Markets Registration" banner. Click "Register TagMarkets".
Important! Do not register directly on the TAG Markets platform — it must be done through the IB Portal so the accounts sync properly.
6
Sync Your Account
A popup will appear: "Welcome to TAG Broker". Enter a password if desired and click "Sync Account".
7
Sync in Progress
Wait while the system verifies your data and syncs your accounts.
8
Sync Successful
You will see the "Sync Successful!" confirmation. TAG Broker will send you an email with further instructions. Click "Close".
9
Verify TAG Markets Email
Open the TAG Markets email. It contains your login credentials and a "Confirm Email Address" button — click it to activate your TAG Markets account.
10
TAG Markets Dashboard
You are now on the TAG Markets dashboard. You will see a notification at the top to complete KYC verification.
11
Start KYC Verification — Sumsub
Click the "upload documents" link on the dashboard. The Sumsub verification popup will appear. Click "Continue".
12
Begin Verification
Click "Start verification". The process consists of two steps: submitting an identity document and completing a biometric verification.
13
Choose Document Type
Select the issuing country and document type (ID card, passport, etc.), then click "Continue".
14
Biometric Verification
Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the biometric verification. Wait while the system reviews your submission.
15
KYC Approved
You will see the message "Your profile has been verified". KYC is successfully completed — you can now make deposits.
16
Dashboard After KYC
After KYC approval, your trading account (MT5, account no. 50627155) is visible on the dashboard. Click "Deposit" to fund your account.
Part 2
Making a Deposit
17
Choose Deposit Amount
Select the currency (USD) and enter your desired amount (e.g. $777) or choose a preset amount ($100, $500, $1000). Click "Next".
18
Choose Payment Method
Select a payment method: Crypto (Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT, etc.) or card/bank transfer (Visa/Mastercard). Click your preferred option.
19
Confirm Crypto Payment
If you chose crypto, the deposit amount ($777) will be displayed. Click "Submit" to continue.
20
Choose Cryptocurrency
Select a cryptocurrency: Bitcoin, Ethereum, Tether (USDT), USD Coin, Binance Coin, or Solana.
We recommend using USDT (Tether) due to low transaction fees.
21
Choose USDT Network
For USDT, we recommend selecting the BSC BEP-20 network due to low fees.
22
Send Payment via QR Code or Address
A payment address and QR code will be displayed. Open your crypto wallet and send the exact amount (777.225395 USDT) to the address shown. The payment expires in approximately 15 minutes.
23
Payment Detected
The system will detect your payment and display the message "Payment found! Awaiting network confirmations". Wait for network confirmations.
Part 3
Connecting to Sonic CopyX Strategy
24
Find CopyX & Browse Traders
On the dashboard, click "CopyX" in the left menu.
25
Browse Traders
The "Browse Pro Traders" page will open, showing all active strategies. Find and click on the "SONIC" strategy.
26
Copy Strategy — Overview
The "Copy Strategy" window will open. Enter your investment amount (min $10). The account type is automatically set to "Amplify".
Important! If the trader loses more than 10% of the initial balance, your account will be liquidated.
27
Copy Strategy — Enter Investment
The "Copy Strategy" window will open. Enter your investment amount (min $10). The account type is automatically set to "Amplify".
Important! If the trader loses more than 10% of the initial balance, your account will be liquidated.
28
Enter Community Token #DW
Click "Community Token" and enter the token code #DW, then click "Verify".
The token changes your Amplify account's drawdown limit to 5% of the initial balance.
29
Enter HASH/GU-ID & Click Copy Now
After the token is verified, the HASH/GU-ID field will appear. Enter the value 1000 and click "Submit". Check both consent boxes and click "Copy Now".
30
CopyX Account Created!
You will see the message "CopyX Account Created". Click "Continue". The SONIC strategy will now automatically copy the trader's trades to your account.
🎉

Congratulations! You're All Set!

You have successfully connected to the SONIC CopyX strategy! From the Followers Hub you can track your strategy's growth, balance, and trades. At the bottom of the page you will also find your MetaTrader 5 login credentials (download the MT5 app from Google Play / App Store) and the server is T.M Financials.
