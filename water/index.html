What's actually in your tap?

These contaminants are legal at low levels. But they accumulate in your body over time.

Pb

Lead

From old pipes. No safe level for children. Affects brain development.

Cl

Chlorine & Byproducts

Disinfection creates THMs linked to cancer risk with long-term exposure.

MP

Microplastics

Found in 83% of tap water worldwide. Not yet regulated or tested.

PF

PFAS "Forever Chemicals"

Never break down. Linked to thyroid disease, cancer, immune issues.

Fe

Rust & Sediment

Iron oxide from corroding pipes. Causes discoloration and metallic taste.

Rx

Pharmaceutical Residues

Hormones, antibiotics, painkillers. Treatment plants can't fully remove them.
